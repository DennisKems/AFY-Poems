FADING

by Abigail Sedinam

Countless times. I've ran away
All to a dead end. But this time,
It's the dead end of a dead end.

No light. No air. No voice.
And I can't run away
Not beacause I do not want to. Not because I can not.
It's just impossibility in itself, I don't know what to do.

Mother! where are you? Please come for my bones
That's if they even remain... Only if you find my ashes
You know when they say: "I am better off dead"
These words are kind.
